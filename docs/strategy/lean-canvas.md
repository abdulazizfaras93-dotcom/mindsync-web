## Wedge Strategy — "Catalog of 8, Hunt in 1"

The website shows all 8 bundles and sets MindSync up as a real agency. Cold outreach for Month 1 is **clinics only**. Reasoning:

1. **Highest retainer** (220–460 KWD/mo) → fastest path to 1,000+ MRR
2. **Clearest ROI narrative** — "one no-show = your retainer paid"
3. **Dense target pool** — ~300+ dental + health clinics in Kuwait mappable via Google Maps/Apify
4. **Warm language** — clinic receptionists are already overwhelmed by WhatsApp inbound; pain is pre-validated
5. **Case-study potential** — appointment-reminder conversion is the easiest automation-ROI story to post on IG

Salons / gyms / garages / restaurants / brokers open in Week 3 once the clinic playbook has 1–2 signed clients as proof.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Land Fast (Phase 1 — NOW)
- Target: First 3 paying **clinic** clients in 30 days
- Method: WhatsApp outreach to ~100 dental + health clinic owners in Kuwait (Week 1 alone)
- Goal: 3 clinic clients × 220 KWD/mo retainer (Smart tier) = 660 KWD MRR as proof point

### 2. Retain + Expand (Month 2–3)
- Every client on maintenance plan from Day 1
- Month 2: Upsell additional agents, social media management, or Meta ads
- Month 3: Referral ask — "Who else do you know running a clinic/salon/gym in Kuwait?"

### 3. Build Authority (Parallel)
- Instagram: 3–4 posts/week — automation demos, client results, bilingual education
- First client result → published case study within 30 days of go-live

### 4. Scale (Month 4–6)
- Hit 10 clients → ~2,100 KWD MRR → exit teaching
- Activate paid Meta ads with proven case study creative
- GCC outreach (UAE clinics, KSA real-estate offices)
