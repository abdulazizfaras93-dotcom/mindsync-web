# MindSync — Validation Playbook
**Date:** 2026-04-20 | **Phase:** 8

Ordered from fastest/cheapest to slowest/most expensive.

---

## Experiment 1 — WhatsApp Direct Outreach (Concierge Pre-Sale)
**Cost:** 0 KWD | **Time:** 1–2 weeks | **Priority:** 🔴 Do first

**Assumption tested:** Kuwait SMB owners will pay for AI automation when shown a live demo.

**How to run:**
1. Scrape 100 restaurant/clinic/salon WhatsApp numbers via Apify (Google Maps Kuwait)
2. Send personalized opener: "Hi [Name], I noticed [Business Name] — I help restaurants/clinics in Kuwait automate their WhatsApp and save 3–4 hours/day. Can I show you a 2-minute demo?"
3. For every positive reply → book a WhatsApp voice/video call
4. Run live demo of Receptionist Agent during call
5. Present Phase 1 pricing — ask for commitment

**Measure:**
- Reply rate (target: ≥15%)
- Demo booked rate (target: ≥40% of replies)
- Close rate (target: ≥30% of demos)
- Days to first paid client

**Validates if:** ≥3 paying clients from first 100 outreach contacts
**Invalidates if:** <5% reply rate AND <1 close from 100 contacts after 2 weeks

## Experiment 2 — Landing Page + "Book a Demo" Test
**Cost:** 0 KWD (Antigravity) | **Time:** 3–5 days to launch | **Priority:** 🔴 Do in parallel

**Assumption tested:** Kuwait SMBs discovering MindSync via Instagram will click through and book a call.

**How to run:**
1. Launch Antigravity landing page — hero + services + CTA ("Book a Free Demo")
2. Post 3 Instagram posts pointing to landing page or WhatsApp
3. Track: page visits, CTA clicks, WhatsApp messages received, calls booked

**Measure:**
- Instagram post → link click rate
- Landing page → booking conversion rate (target: ≥5%)
- WhatsApp DM volume from Instagram

**Validates if:** ≥5 inbound demo requests in 30 days from organic Instagram
**Invalidates if:** Zero inbound after 30 days + 10 posts

## Experiment 3 — Discovery Call Script Test
**Cost:** 0 KWD | **Time:** Ongoing | **Priority:** 🔴 Do from Week 1

**Assumption tested:** The discovery call → proposal → close sequence works within 2 weeks per client.

**How to run:**
1. Use structured call framework: Pain → Current solution → Cost of problem → MindSync solution → Pricing → Next step
2. Track every call: outcome, objections raised, time to close
3. Iterate script after every 5 calls

**Measure:**
- Demo-to-proposal rate (target: ≥80%)
- Proposal-to-close rate (target: ≥40%)
- Average sales cycle length (target: <14 days)
- Top 3 objections raised

**Validates if:** Close rate ≥30% after 10 demos
**Invalidates if:** Close rate <10% after 20 demos despite multiple script iterations

## Experiment 4 — Retainer Stickiness Test
**Cost:** 0 KWD | **Time:** Month 2–3 | **Priority:** 🟡 After first clients

**Assumption tested:** Clients will stay on maintenance retainers month-to-month without churn.

**How to run:**
1. Onboard first 3 clients
2. At Day 30, send performance report via Client Portal
3. At Day 45, schedule a 15-min check-in call
4. At Day 60, ask: "What's working? What else could we automate?"
5. Track: renewal rate, upsell rate, NPS score

**Measures:**
- Month 2 renewal rate (target: 100% in first 3 months)
- NPS (target: ≥8/10)
- Upsell rate (target: ≥30% add a module by Month 3)

**Validates if:** All 3 first clients renew at Month 2
**Invalidates if:** 2+ clients cancel before Month 3

## Experiment 5 — GCC Remote Sales Test
**Cost:** 0 KWD | **Time:** Month 3–4 | **Priority:** 🟢 After Kuwait validation

**Assumption tested:** GCC SMBs (UAE, KSA) will buy remotely from a Kuwait-based agency.

**How to run:**
1. Identify 20 UAE clinics or KSA restaurants via LinkedIn/Instagram
2. Send bilingual (EN/AR) outreach message
3. Run video demo call
4. Track: reply rate vs. Kuwait rate, close rate vs. Kuwait rate, objections specific to "not local"

**Validates if:** ≥1 GCC client closes within 60 days of outreach start
**Invalidates if:** 0 closes from 50 GCC contacts — indicates local presence needed before GCC expansion

## Experiment 6 — Paid Meta Ads Test
**Cost:** 200–300 KWD | **Time:** 2–3 weeks | **Priority:** 🟢 Month 4+ only

**Assumption tested:** Paid Instagram/Facebook ads drive qualified inbound leads at acceptable CAC.

**How to run:**
1. Create 2 ad variants: one targeting restaurant owners, one targeting clinic managers
2. Drive to landing page booking form or WhatsApp
3. Budget: 150 KWD per variant (300 KWD total)
4. Run for 2 weeks

**Validates if:** ≥3 demos booked at <50 KWD CAC (per demo)
**Invalidates if:** <1 demo booked per 100 KWD spent — organic-only strategy needed

## Discovery Call Script Template

```
Opening (2 min):
"Hi [Name], thanks for connecting. I'm from MindSync — 
we help Kuwait [restaurants/clinics/salons] automate their WhatsApp 
and operations with AI. I wanted to understand your situation first 
before showing you anything."

Pain discovery (5 min):
1. "How are you currently handling booking/inquiries — phone, WhatsApp, walk-in?"
2. "Roughly how many messages/calls do you get per day?"
3. "What happens when you or your staff miss one?"
4. "Do you know which customers came back vs. came once and never returned?"

Current solution (2 min):
5. "What have you tried to solve this? Any tools, staff, systems?"
6. "What's worked, what hasn't?"

Cost of problem (2 min):
7. "If I told you 30% of the inquiries you're missing are turning into your competitor's customers — does that feel accurate?"
8. "What would it mean to your business to never miss a message again?"

Demo (5 min):
"Let me show you exactly what we'd build for you..."
[Live demo of Receptionist Agent via WhatsApp or web]

Offer (3 min):
"For a [restaurant/clinic] like yours, we'd typically start with [Bundle A/B/C].
That's [X KWD] one-time setup, then [Y KWD/month] to keep it running.
In Phase 1, we include [Free bonuses] at no cost.
Does that feel like something worth moving forward on?"

Close:
"If yes — I can have you live in 10 business days. All I need is 
[WhatsApp number, business info, branding assets]."
```
