# MindSync — Kill Criteria
**Date:** 2026-04-20 | **Phase:** 8

These are the specific, measurable conditions under which you stop or pivot. Review monthly.

---

| # | Kill Condition | Measurement | Linked Experiment | Action if Triggered |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Fewer than 1 paying client after 60 days of active outreach (100+ contacts reached) | Signed contracts + invoices issued | Experiment 1 | Pivot: Change ICP (try retail instead of F&B), OR drop price to 100 KWD for first 3 clients, OR offer free pilot |
| 2 | Demo-to-close rate below 10% after 20 demos despite 3+ script iterations | CRM close rate tracking | Experiment 3 | Pivot: Problem not felt urgently enough — try different vertical or reframe offer |
| 3 | Fewer than 3 of first 5 clients renew their maintenance plan at Month 2 | Renewal rate in Sheets CRM | Experiment 4 | Pivot: Retainer model broken — either value isn't felt OR wrong pricing — restructure maintenance deliverables |
| 4 | Average time-to-deliver exceeds 15 business days consistently (3+ projects) | Delivery timeline tracking | Internal | Scope down service offering — Basic only until capacity issue resolved |
| 5 | Monthly revenue below 500 KWD at Month 4 (conservative scenario lower bound) | MRR tracking | Financial model | Stop full-time transition plans — stay in teaching until Month 6 reassessment |
| 6 | Zero GCC closes from 50+ outreach contacts at Month 5 | GCC pipeline in CRM | Experiment 5 | Kill GCC ambition for Year 1 — refocus 100% on Kuwait until 15 clients |
| 7 | More than 2 client complaints about agent accuracy or system downtime in first 3 months | Support ticket log | Client Portal | Pause new sales — fix delivery quality first before more clients |

## Pivot Options (if kill criteria triggered)

| Trigger | Pivot Option |
|---|---|
| No closes (Kill #1, #2) | Niche down to ONE vertical only (clinics) — laser-focused positioning |
| Retainer churn (Kill #3) | Switch to annual contracts with upfront payment discount |
| Delivery overload (Kill #4) | Raise prices 2x to reduce volume while preserving revenue |
| Revenue too slow (Kill #5) | Add a productized "done-in-a-day" WhatsApp setup (flat 150 KWD) — quick cash while building retainer base |
| GCC fails (Kill #6) | Double down Kuwait — 20+ clients before any geographic expansion |
