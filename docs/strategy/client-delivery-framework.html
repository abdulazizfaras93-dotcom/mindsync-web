PART 01 The Discovery Call Guide

Run through this on WhatsApp or a call. ~15–20 minutes. Client talks, MindSync notes. No forms for the client to fill. After this call you have everything to fill the Build Brief and write the proposal.

Block 1 2 min

Business Identity

  • Business name + industry
  • How long have they been operating?
  • Owner-operated, or do they have staff? How many?
  • Primary language with customers — Arabic, English, or both?
  • Which platforms are they active on? (WhatsApp, Instagram, website, Google Maps, Snapchat, TikTok)
Block 2 5 min

The Pain

  • How do customers contact you today? (WhatsApp, phone, walk-in, Instagram DMs)
  • How many messages or calls do you get per day roughly?
  • Who replies — you, a staff member, or does it go unanswered?
  • What happens when you miss a message?
  • Do you follow up with customers after they visit?
  • Have you tried any tool, app, or staff to fix this? What happened?
Block 3 5 min

Business Content

  • List your services and their prices (or "send me your menu / price list")
  • Working hours — daily schedule including weekends
  • Address / area (for Google Maps link in agent responses)
  • Any active promotions or seasonal offers?
  • What should the agent never say or offer?
  • How do you currently handle bookings? (WhatsApp, Google Calendar, booking app, walk-in)
  • Agent confirms bookings directly, or collects request and staff follows up?
Block 4 3 min

Channels & Platforms

  • Do you have a WhatsApp Business account (not personal WhatsApp)?
  • Do you manage your own Instagram DMs, or does someone else?
  • Do you have a website? If yes, what's the URL?
  • Do you use any booking system, CRM, or Google Calendar?
  • Do you have a Google account linked to the business?

If Website ordered:

  • Photos, logo, and written content ready — or do you need help?
  • Any website you liked the look of? (Send me a link)
  • Main job of the website — show services, take bookings, or both?

If Mobile App ordered:

  • Who is the app for — customers, staff, or both?
  • The one thing the app must do? (Bookings / Loyalty / Ordering / Tracking)
  • iOS, Android, or both?
Block 5 2 min

Expectations & Access

  • What's the #1 thing you want handled automatically?
  • Any concerns about an AI agent talking to your customers?
  • Is there a deadline or upcoming season driving the go-live date?
  • Who will be the point of contact for approvals during the build?
End of call — say this: "After we agree to work together, I'll send you a short checklist on WhatsApp — things like your Meta Business account access, logo file, and a few logins. Most of it takes 5 minutes to gather. I'll walk you through anything that's new to you."

PART 03 The Delivery Roadmap

AI Agent is the core track. Website and App are parallel tracks that sync at go-live. All active tracks run simultaneously.

PHASE 0 Kickoff — Day 0 Triggered when contract signed + 50% upfront received
  • Send client "We've started" WhatsApp message
  • Create client's Project in self-hosted n8n (agents.mindsynckw.com)
  • Create client's Google Sheet (copy master template)
  • Fill Build Brief Parts A–F
  • Collect all credentials from Part F checklist
  • If no Meta Business API: start verification immediately (1–3 days)
  • Duplicate Universal Receptionist into client's Project → set webhook path to /webhook/receptionist-{clientslug}
  • Open active tracks: AI Agent ✅ | Website (if ordered) | App (if ordered)
  • Add client to Admin Dashboard
PHASE 1 Build — Days 1–X All active tracks run in parallel
AI AGENT TRACK
  • Configure ⚙️ CLIENT CONFIG node from Part B
  • Load knowledge base: services, hours, FAQs, tone, escalation
  • Connect channels: WhatsApp, Instagram, website widget
  • Internal test: 10 scenarios in both languages
WEBSITE TRACK
  • Scaffold Next.js from MindSync template
  • Build sections from Part C scope
  • Embed AI Agent chat widget
  • Internal review before showing client
APP TRACK
  • Scaffold from MindSync app template
  • Build core feature from Part D
  • Connect to agent backend
  • Internal review before showing client
CHECK 1 Client Review Max 1 round · max 3 revision items per track · scope locks after this
  • AI Agent: give client a test WhatsApp number to message freely
  • Website: share Netlify preview link
  • App: share TestFlight link (iOS) or APK (Android)
  • Collect feedback · address all items before Phase 2
PHASE 2 Refine & Connect Apply feedback + wire all tracks together
  • Apply all Checkpoint 1 feedback across active tracks
  • Wire: Website chat widget → AI Agent
  • Wire: App bookings → Google Calendar / agent backend
  • Wire: All channels → unified Google Sheet CRM log
  • End-to-end test: full customer journey across all active channels
  • Prepare go-live checklist
CHECK 2 Final Sign-Off 15-min WhatsApp call · invoice 2nd 50% · collect before activating
  • Full live walkthrough of the connected system
  • Staff micro-training: what the agent does, escalates, how to read the Sheet
  • Client confirms verbally or via WhatsApp
  • Collect 50% final payment before go-live activation
PHASE 3 Go-Live First retainer charge processed on this day
  • Activate all n8n workflows on agents.mindsynckw.com
  • Point website domain (if applicable)
  • Submit app to App Store / Google Play (if applicable)
  • Monitor first 48 hours — Google Sheet for activity, errors, escalations
  • Send client "You're live" WhatsApp with Client Portal link
  • Update client status to Active in Admin Dashboard
