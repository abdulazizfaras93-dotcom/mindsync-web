# MindSync — WhatsApp Outreach Templates (Cold)

All messages follow the same structure:
1. **Opening** — personal observation (shows you're not spam)
2. **Curiosity question** — targets their specific daily pain
3. **Tease the solution** — one line, no price, no feature dump
4. **Soft CTA** — lowest friction response possible

Keep it under 5 lines. Never pitch on first contact. Goal = get a reply.

## Rules for Sending

1. **Always personalize the opening** — mention their actual business name or something specific from their Instagram/profile. Never send a generic "صاحب/ة" opener.
2. **Send one at a time** — don't blast. WhatsApp bans accounts that send identical messages to many numbers at once from a personal number.
3. **Best time:** Sunday–Thursday, 10am–12pm Kuwait time (before the lunch rush).
4. **Track every send** in the outreach spreadsheet (Name · Number · Sent Date · Status).
5. **Never pitch price on first contact.** Price comes after the discovery call.
6. **If they reply with anything positive** → move immediately to booking a 20-min call.
